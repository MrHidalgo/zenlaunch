ZEN LAUNCH TREASURY SYSTEM

Zen Launch will receive it’s launchpad fees in tokens as well as use reserves to participate in presale rounds on listed projects These tokens will be held in treasury for a minimum of 12 months, staked and farmed where applicable, providing an added underlying value to back the ZENL token

This aligns Zen, it’s project and IDO participants in the long-term health of the projects on Zen Launch by reducing selling pressure.

As our projects are successful and generate high ROI’s, our purchasing power increases and treasury begins to grow exponentially.

As this approach compounds over time, Zen Launch’s treasury backed $ZENL token, becomes the launchpad gold-standard for excellence.
