PROBLEMS & OPPORTUNITIES

We believe in the long-term prospects for the launchpad niche but also feel the space offers room for significant improvements.

Capital sits dormant in CEX’s because people are afraid to move to decentralized platforms. They find it overwhelming and feel uncomfortable with unprofessional presentations. We see opportunity to improve the user experience.

Existing launchpads are incentivized to list any project they can to generate revenue. The “launch and leave” mentality is obvious to the sophisticated end-user. We can create better value.

The user has little loyalty. They shop around and dump their token after they get the deal they wanted. This destabilizes the launchpad token value and diminishes value. We see this an opportunity to create outsized value for our seed/private investors relative to other launchpads.

SIGNAL VS. NOISE

Of the top launchpads by marketcap:

  • Launchpad and DeFi are often conflated on website lists (RAY,SUPER, BOUNCE, XTM)
  • Many are exclusively gaming (UFO, SFUND, GAFI, GZONE)
  • Several are chain-specific and require a user to bridge. This is a non-starter for HNWI users. (BSCPAD, XAVA, ADAPAD, PLSPAD, KCCPAD)
  • Several overwhelm with a crowded menu, multiple functionalities. , launchpad services are non-obvious (PAID, SWAP, DDIM)
  • Most list too many games, not enough value.

A high net-worth user is unlikely to sifting through confusing options.

When overwhelmed, they’ll err on the side of caution and not participate at all.

We are solving for this key pain point.

CONCENTRATING ON VALUE

We believe in the long-term prospects for the launchpad niche but also feel the space offers room for significant improvements.

  • Polkastarter lists 90 completed projects. 28 projects had an ATH >3000% of IDO price. 22 of these projects were non-gaming related.
  • DAOMaker lists 87 completed projects. 11 projects did 70-352x ATH’s from IDO. Only two of these projects were games. (Also notable - launchpad hosting their IDO’s here outperformed and had 3 spots on this list)
  • Out of Trustswap’s top 10 performing projects, only 2 were gaming/NFT related.

Money-grab projects dilute returns and create too much noise. Smart users know this. There is a massive opportunity for more targeted offerings.

At Zen Launch, we believe less is more. High-end users don’t want to shop in a flea market. They want a more carefully curated experience. That holds true in fine-dining, high fashion and finance. That key element of consumer behavior will continue to hold true on the Web3 frontier as well. Our approach is to carefully select for the best projects and build trust with our users. By taking a long view of the market, we believe strongly that will position Zen Launch to capture increasing value over time.

KEY STRATEGY POINTS

Zen Launch has a number of key strategical differences in its approach:

  • We will release only 1-2 high quality projects per month, making each launch an ‘event’ and giving each project plenty of time to engage our users in advance.
  • Our coinvestment & treasury strategy is a value-add for our projects’s tokenomics and the value of our own token.
  • IDO allotment is based on staking tiers and runs in 20 cycles (10 days before to whitelist, 10 days lock after if allotment received). This reduces volatility of our token and favors large holders.
  • Team will establish multiple vendor partnerships and offer advising, tokenomics support and more to our launchpad projects.
  • We will seek to engage entrepreneurs in our network to bring their ideas on-chain and facilitate connections with designer & developers. This creates a non-competitive deal flow and distinguishes us with very unique launch offerings.
  • Community engagement further strengthened through DAO-like Discord activity including access to trading alpha, syndicated private rounds of other deals, etc.

ZEN LAUNCH TREASURY SYSTEM

Zen Launch will receive it’s launchpad fees in tokens as well as use reserves to participate in presale rounds on listed projects These tokens will be held in treasury for a minimum of 12 months, staked and farmed where applicable, providing an added underlying value to back the ZENL token

This aligns Zen, it’s project and IDO participants in the long-term health of the projects on Zen Launch by reducing selling pressure.

As our projects are successful and generate high ROI’s, our purchasing power increases and treasury begins to grow exponentially.

As this approach compounds over time, Zen Launch’s treasury backed $ZENL token, becomes the launchpad gold-standard for excellence.
